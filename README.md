<!-- ================= EPISODE 1 ================= -->
<h2 id="ep1">Episode 1 – Farkon Tafiya</h2>

<h3 id="ep1A">Part A – Farkon Tafiya</h3>
<p>
Amina ta tashi da safe, hasken rana na shiga dakinta. Zuciyarta cike da bege da tsoro. Ta tuna maganar mahaifiyarta: "Diyata, gaskiya da jajircewa sune makullin rayuwa." 
</p>
<p>
Ta shirya jakar ta, ta yi bankwana da mahaifi da ‘yan uwanta. Kowanne daga cikin su na yi mata addu’a. Tafiyarta zuwa birni babban canji ne a rayuwarta.
</p>
<p>
Lokacin da ta isa, birnin ya yi matuƙar girma. Motoci, mutane, kasuwanni — duk suna ƙara jin daɗi amma kuma suna cike da tsoro. 
</p>

<h3 id="ep1B">Part B – Hanyoyi Masu Tabbas</h3>
<p>
A makaranta, Amina ta haɗu da sabbin abokai. Fatima da Suleiman sun zama masu goyon baya. Amma Hauwa na kallonta da hassada. Duk da haka, Amina ta jajirce, ta fara samun karbuwa a aji.
</p>
<p>
Sakamakon farko ya fito. Sunanta na sama a allo. Farin ciki ya cika zuciyarta, amma ta sani akwai waɗanda ba za su yi farin ciki ba.
</p>

<h3 id="ep1C">Part C – Sabuwar Fara</h3>
<p>
Daren farko a gidan abokin karatu, ta zauna a ɗaki tana rubutu. Ta fahimci cewa rayuwa ta fara, kuma kowanne mataki na da darasi.  
</p>

<!-- ================= EPISODE 2 ================= -->
<h2 id="ep2">Episode 2 – Nasara da Nisa</h2>

<h3 id="ep2A">Part A – Sabuwar Rayuwa</h3>
<p>
Bayan hutun summer, Amina ta dawo makaranta. Sabon aji, sabbin kalubale. Ta hadu da manyan jarrabawa, amma haɗin kai da goyon bayan abokai sun taimaka mata.
</p>
<p>
Duk da hassada da aka yi mata, ta fara samun nasara a kowane gwaji. Fatima ta tsaya kusa da ita, tana ba da shawara da goyon baya.
</p>

<h3 id="ep2B">Part B – Jarabawar Nasara</h3>
<p>
Gasarta ta fara lokacin babban jarabawa. Hauwa ta yi ƙoƙarin cutar da ita, amma Amina ta gano wannan cikin hikima.  
</p>
<p>
Ta kammala aikin ta da kyau, ta lashe gasar. Malamai da dalibai sun yaba mata. Ta koyi cewa gaskiya da aiki tukuru suna kawo nasara fiye da kowanne sirri ko mugunta.
</p>

<h3 id="ep2C">Part C – Farkon Haske</h3>
<p>
Bayan gasar, Amina ta koma gida tare da Fatima da Suleiman. Mahaifiyarta ta rungume ta da ƙarfi. Ta fahimci cewa kowanne ƙaramin aiki na gaskiya yana haifar da babban haske a rayuwa.
</p>

<h2 id="ep3">Episode 3 – Ci gaba da Sirrin Rayuwa</h2>

<h3 id="ep3A">Part A – Sabuwar Ƙalubale</h3>
<p>
Amina ta shiga sabon ajin da babban ƙalubale. Mr. Okoye ya sanar da babban gasar ilimi mai zuwa. Kyautar tafiya zuwa Jamus. Wannan dama ce ta rayuwa amma tana da wahala sosai.
</p>

<h3 id="ep3B">Part B – Ƙarshen Sirri</h3>
<p>
Hauwa ta yi ƙoƙarin cutar da Amina, amma Amina ta cika da hikima da haɗin kai tare da Fatima da Suleiman. Sun ci gaba da lashe gasar cikin nasara.
</p>

<h3 id="ep3C">Part C – Farkon Haske</h3>
<p>
Bayan gasar, Amina ta koma gida, ta rungume mahaifiyarta, ta fahimci nasara ba kawai samun kyauta ba ce, amma riƙe gaskiya da aiki tukuru.
</p>
